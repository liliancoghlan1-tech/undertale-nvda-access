============================================================
 UNDERTALE - NVDA ACCESSIBILITY MOD - KEY REFERENCE  (v1.6)
============================================================

Always have NVDA running before you start the game.
Text, menus, and events are spoken automatically.

NEW IN THIS VERSION: press H at any time to hear the hint for
whatever you are currently doing (dodging, jumping, a puzzle,
the storage box...). If you only remember one key, remember H.

------------------------------------------------------------
TWO THINGS THAT CONFUSE EVERYONE - READ THIS FIRST
------------------------------------------------------------
1. ONE PRESS, DON'T MASH. Every guided action in this mod is
   a single press. Pressing a direction while dodging JUMPS
   your soul to the safe spot - holding or mashing it fights
   the mod and gets you hit. Press once, listen, press again.

2. PITCH MEANS TWO DIFFERENT THINGS, depending on what you
   are doing. This is the single most common confusion:
     - When you ATTACK (the FIGHT bar): pitch means TIMING.
       Rising, faster beeps = the target is coming. Press Z
       on the highest, fastest beep.
     - When you DODGE (the bullet part): pitch means HEIGHT.
       High = the safe spot is near the TOP of the box, low =
       near the BOTTOM. It is not telling you "press now".
   In both cases, which EAR you hear it in = left or right.

------------------------------------------------------------
WALKING AROUND (the overworld)
------------------------------------------------------------
  Arrow keys or W A S D ... move
  Z ....................... talk / interact / read / confirm
  X ....................... cancel / back
  C ....................... open your menu (Items, Stats, Cell)

  As you walk, the mod tells you the direction and number of
  steps to your current target, and says "Blocked" at walls.

  E ....................... scan: lists the nearest exit, save
                            point, person, sign, switch, etc.
  T ....................... select the next nearby thing
  R ....................... select the previous nearby thing
  G ....................... repeat your current target
  U ....................... EXITS ONLY: cycle just the ways out
                            of this room, nearest first, with the
                            compass direction and distance. This
                            also sets it as your target, so you
                            can press V straight after to walk
                            there. Use this when you are lost.
  V ....................... auto-walk to your selected target
                            (press V again, or any move key, to stop)
  L ....................... look: describe the area you are in
                            (repeats that room's audio description)
  H ....................... repeat the hint for what you are doing

  As you walk you also hear soft footsteps, a thud at walls, a
  door sound through doorways, and a bell when something you can
  interact with is right next to you.

------------------------------------------------------------
THE PUZZLE SKIP  (P and O)
------------------------------------------------------------
  P ....................... hear the exits / press again to cycle
  O ....................... go through the chosen exit

  This works in any room, as an escape hatch if you are ever
  truly stuck. But you should NOT need it often - almost every
  puzzle in the game is playable by ear with the guidance the
  mod gives you.

  There are exactly THREE puzzles that are genuinely visual and
  that the mod itself will tell you to skip:
     - Waterfall: the bridge-seed puzzle (seeds drifting in
       water that sprout into lily-pad bridges).
     - Hotland: the shooting puzzle.
     - Hotland: the steam-vent / bounce-pad and conveyor puzzle
       (the way onward there is the door to the EAST).
  In each of those rooms the description tells you to press P
  then O. Everything else is meant to be played.

------------------------------------------------------------
ACCESSIBILITY MENU and MUSIC (anywhere)
------------------------------------------------------------
  K ....................... open the Accessibility menu. Use Up
                            and Down to move, Left and Right to
                            change a setting, K or X to close.
                            Turn features on or off (screen
                            reading, scanning, puzzle skip,
                            navigation sounds, combat cues), set
                            the difficulty mode, and set music
                            volume. Your choices are saved.
                            (You can also open it from the title
                            screen: choose "Accessibility options".)
  N ....................... music quieter
  B ....................... music louder

------------------------------------------------------------
BATTLES - the menus (FIGHT / ACT / ITEM / MERCY)
------------------------------------------------------------
  Arrow keys .............. move the cursor (options are read)
  Z ....................... choose
  X ....................... go back
  D ....................... describe the monster you are facing -
                            what it looks like. Free; costs no turn.

  When you FIGHT, you hear quick "parking-sensor" beeps that
  speed up and rise in pitch toward the center - press Z on
  the fastest, highest beep for the best hit. (Pitch = timing
  here. See the note at the top.)

------------------------------------------------------------
BATTLES - dodging attacks (the bullet/heart part)
------------------------------------------------------------
  M ....................... change difficulty mode. It cycles:
        Assisted = you cannot be defeated (recommended)
        Slow     = half speed, but you can be defeated
        Normal   = full speed, you can be defeated
  A guide beep plays toward the SAFEST SPOT in the box:
        which ear  = it is to your left or your right
        high / low pitch = it is near the top or the bottom
  Press the matching Arrow (or W A S D) ONCE and your soul
  jumps onto that spot. You will hear a quick two-tone chime
  when you land, so you always know the jump worked. If you
  hear a single low tone instead, there was nowhere safe to
  land - wait for the wave to change.
  Q ....................... say where your heart is in the box
  F (hold) ................ fast-forward through slow moments
  H ....................... repeat this explanation in game

  FRIENDLY THINGS YOU SHOULD TOUCH: some monsters throw things
  that HEAL you and are what makes them spareable (Vegetoid's
  vegetables, Woshua, Whimsalot). Those play a WARM RISING
  DOUBLE CHIRP instead of a hazard beep, and the mod will not
  steer you away from them. Move ONTO a double chirp.

  BLUE heart (Papyrus): you stand on a floor and JUMP over bones.
  Press Up to jump - the mod sets the height for you, so you only
  have to TIME it. A beep pans to the side each bone comes from and
  speeds up as it nears; press Up when it peaks. The pitch tells you
  the bone: a HIGH beep is a small bone (quick hop), a LOW beep is a
  tall bone (big jump), and a slow low pulse means a bone overhead -
  stay down, don't jump. A soft tick = you've landed, jump the next.

  GREEN heart (Undyne): you hold a shield and face incoming spears.
  A beep tells you the side - press that Arrow to block it.

------------------------------------------------------------
SHOPS, STORAGE AND SPECIAL BITS
------------------------------------------------------------
  Shops ................... every option is read. In the Temmie
                            shop, SELLING is now read too: the
                            item, what she will pay, and when
                            she will not buy something at all.
  Storage box ............. the dimensional box is read. Left and
                            Right swap between what you are
                            carrying and what is in the box, Up
                            and Down move through the slots, Z
                            moves an item across.
  Mettaton quiz show ...... the question and all 4 answers are
                            read. Press 1, 2, 3 or 4 to pick,
                            then Z to lock it in. No time limit.
  Elevators ............... use the Arrow keys and Z; every
                            option is read aloud.

------------------------------------------------------------
THE METTATON MINIGAMES (new in v1.6)
------------------------------------------------------------
  These used to be dead ends. Press H in any of them to hear
  the full explanation again.

  Cooking-show jetpack climb: you rise automatically - you only
  steer LEFT and RIGHT. A beep points to the CLEAR side and its
  pitch rises as a light gets close. You are told the time left
  and how far up you are.

  Coloured tile maze: walk the grid to the right-hand side.
  Q ....................... scan the four tiles around you
  Pink is safe, red is a wall, yellow zaps you, green is
  harmless. Orange tiles make you smell of oranges, purple tiles
  make you smell of lemons, and water is safe UNLESS you smell
  of oranges. So step on a purple tile before crossing water.
  (This puzzle is meant to be hard - that part is not the mod.)

  News report bomb defusal: the six bombs are now announced by
  the scanner. Press E to scan, T and R to pick one, V to walk
  to it. While defusing, a beep tracks the bomb piece as it
  sweeps side to side; choose the defuse option the instant you
  hear the sharp high tone. You are told how many bombs remain.

------------------------------------------------------------
FINAL BOSS - Photoshop Flowey
------------------------------------------------------------
  Keep Assisted mode on (press M until "you cannot be defeated").
  When you hear "Soul round. Press Z to free this soul":
  Z ....................... free the soul (no aiming needed!)
                            Do this for all 6 souls.
  After "All six souls freed", press Z to attack with the
  fight button and finish him.
  R ....................... auto-free the current soul
  O ....................... say the current state (souls freed,
                            HP, whether a soul round is active)
  F (hold) ................ speed up the quiet stretches
