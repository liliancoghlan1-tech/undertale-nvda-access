============================================================
 UNDERTALE - NVDA ACCESSIBILITY MOD - KEY REFERENCE
============================================================

Always have NVDA running before you start the game.
Text, menus, and events are spoken automatically.

------------------------------------------------------------
WALKING AROUND (the overworld)
------------------------------------------------------------
  Arrow keys or W A S D ... move
  Z ....................... talk / interact / read / confirm
  X ....................... cancel / back
  C ....................... open your menu (Items, Stats, Cell)

  As you walk, the mod tells you the direction and number of
  steps to your current target, and says "Blocked" at walls.

  E ....................... scan: lists the nearest exit, save
                            point, person, sign, switch, etc.
  T ....................... select the next nearby thing
  R ....................... select the previous nearby thing
  G ....................... repeat your current target
  V ....................... auto-walk to your selected target
                            (press V again, or any move key, to stop)

  As you walk you also hear soft footsteps, a thud at walls, a
  door sound through doorways, and a bell when something you can
  interact with is right next to you.

  Stuck on a puzzle that needs eyesight (and only then):
  P ....................... skip-puzzle mode / cycle the exits
  O ....................... confirm the chosen exit

------------------------------------------------------------
ACCESSIBILITY MENU and MUSIC (anywhere)
------------------------------------------------------------
  K ....................... open the Accessibility menu. Use Up
                            and Down to move, Left and Right to
                            change a setting, K or X to close.
                            Turn features on or off (screen
                            reading, scanning, puzzle skip,
                            navigation sounds, combat cues), set
                            the difficulty mode, and set music
                            volume. Your choices are saved.
                            (You can also open it from the title
                            screen: choose "Accessibility options".)
  N ....................... music quieter
  B ....................... music louder

------------------------------------------------------------
BATTLES - the menus (FIGHT / ACT / ITEM / MERCY)
------------------------------------------------------------
  Arrow keys .............. move the cursor (options are read)
  Z ....................... choose
  X ....................... go back

  When you FIGHT, you hear quick "parking-sensor" beeps that
  speed up and rise in pitch toward the center - press Z on
  the fastest, highest beep for the best hit.

------------------------------------------------------------
BATTLES - dodging attacks (the bullet/heart part)
------------------------------------------------------------
  M ....................... change difficulty mode. It cycles:
        Assisted = you cannot be defeated (recommended)
        Slow     = half speed, but you can be defeated
        Normal   = full speed, you can be defeated
  A guide beep plays toward the safe spot:
        which ear  = move left or right
        high / low pitch = move up or down
  Press the matching Arrow (or W A S D) to jump to the safe spot.
  Q ....................... say where your heart is in the box
  F (hold) ................ fast-forward through slow moments

  BLUE heart (Papyrus): you stand on a floor and JUMP over bones.
  Press Up to jump - the mod sets the height for you, so you only
  have to TIME it. A beep pans to the side each bone comes from and
  speeds up as it nears; press Up when it peaks. The pitch tells you
  the bone: a HIGH beep is a small bone (quick hop), a LOW beep is a
  tall bone (big jump), and a slow low pulse means a bone overhead -
  stay down, don't jump. A soft tick = you've landed, jump the next.

  GREEN heart (Undyne): you hold a shield and face incoming spears.
  A beep tells you the side - press that Arrow to block it.

------------------------------------------------------------
SPECIAL BITS
------------------------------------------------------------
  Mettaton quiz show ...... the question and all 4 answers are
                            read. Press 1, 2, 3 or 4 to pick,
                            then Z to lock it in. No time limit.
  Hotland shooting puzzle . press P, then O, to skip it.
  Elevators and shops ..... use the Arrow keys and Z; every
                            option is read aloud.

------------------------------------------------------------
FINAL BOSS - Photoshop Flowey
------------------------------------------------------------
  Keep Assisted mode on (press M until "you cannot be defeated").
  When you hear "Soul round. Press Z to free this soul":
  Z ....................... free the soul (no aiming needed!)
                            Do this for all 6 souls.
  After "All six souls freed", press Z to attack with the
  fight button and finish him.
  R ....................... auto-free the current soul
  O ....................... say the current state (souls freed,
                            HP, whether a soul round is active)
  F (hold) ................ speed up the quiet stretches
