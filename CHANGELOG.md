# Changelog

## v1.5 — "Audio description & a real sense of place" (2026-07-08)

The biggest update yet. Until now the mod made everything **playable** by ear; this update
makes the world **describable** by ear. If you're blind, you now get the things a sighted
player just *sees* — who's on screen, what a room looks like, and what happens in the silent
moments between the dialogue.

### 🆕 Audio description (brand new)
- **Character descriptions.** The first time you meet each major character, you hear what they
  look like — Flowey, Toriel, Sans, Papyrus, Undyne, Alphys, Asgore, Mettaton and more.
  Appearance first, no spoilers.
- **Every room described.** Walk into any room in the game — Ruins, Snowdin, Waterfall,
  Hotland, the CORE, the True Lab, New Home — and hear a short description of what's around
  you the first time you arrive. Press **L** any time to hear the current room again.
- **Cutscene descriptions.** The silent, visual story beats are now narrated: Flowey's turn,
  Toriel's rescue and her goodbye, Undyne's chase across the bridge, Mettaton's dramatic
  transformation, Asgore in the throne room, the six souls, the SAVE being torn apart, and
  the final boss reveal — all the moments that used to happen in silence.
- **Monster descriptions — press D in battle.** Hear what the monster you're facing actually
  looks like (Undertale is about sparing these creatures — now you can picture them). It's
  free and **does not cost a turn.**
- **Speaker names.** Dialogue now tells you *who* is speaking when it changes.

### 🚶 Auto-walk, rebuilt
- Auto-walk (**V**) now reliably routes **around** walls, furniture and obstacles and takes
  corners cleanly, instead of grinding into them. It heads for your selected target and
  slides along walls rather than getting stuck.

### ♿ Also
- Braille display support (from v1.4) rides along with all of the above.
- New keys: **D** (describe monster, in battle) and **L** (describe the room, in the overworld).

## v1.5-es — Edición en español (2026-07-08)

The Spanish edition catches all the way up: the **entire** mod is now translated to Castilian
Spanish, not just the menus. Every character description, every room description, every
cutscene, and all ~50 monster descriptions are now in Spanish, layered on top of the community
Spanish translation **"Undertale Español Castellano" by ArceUseless**. (Story dialogue is
ArceUseless's translation; the accessibility narration is ours.)
